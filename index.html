Appetizers

Samosa

A crispy, spiced potato-filled pastry, perfect as a starter.

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Oil for frying

Pakora

Deep-fried fritters made with vegetables and chickpea flour.

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 potato, sliced
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Oil for frying

Main Courses

Butter Chicken

Creamy, tomato-based curry with tender chicken pieces.

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste

Paneer Tikka

Marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection.

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Skewers for grilling

Chicken Biryani

Fragrant rice dish layered with spiced chicken and saffron.

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 tsp saffron
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices: cloves, cinnamon, cardamom

Desserts

Ras Malai

Soft cheese dumplings soaked in sweetened cardamom syrup.

  • 1 liter milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cardamom powder
  • 4 cups water
  • Pistachios for garnish

Gulab Jamun

Sweet milk dumplings soaked in rose-flavored syrup.

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp rose essence
  • Oil for frying

Beverages

Masala Chai

Spiced tea brewed with aromatic Indian spices.

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tsp tea leaves
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • Sugar to taste

Sweet Lassi

Creamy yogurt-based drink, refreshing and sweet.

  • 2 cups yogurt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Ice cubes
  • Pistachios for garnish
